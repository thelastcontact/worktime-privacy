Privacy Policy for Work Time

Last updated: December 2025

The Work Time application does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All information entered into the app, including shift schedules, notes, statistics, and preferences, remains stored only on your device.

Data Not Collected

  • No personal data is collected.
  • No data is transmitted to external servers.
  • No usage analytics are stored.
  • No information is shared with third parties.

In-App Purchases

The app includes optional auto-renewable subscriptions (premium monthly or yearly). Payments are handled securely through the Apple App Store. The app does not have access to payment information.

Crash Data

Crash information may be collected by Apple automatically to improve stability. This data is not accessible to the developer and is not linked to your identity.

Your Rights

Since the app does not collect any personal data, there is no stored information to access, modify, or delete.
